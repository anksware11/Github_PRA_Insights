# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI GitHub PR Analyzer (the "Service") collects, uses, and protects information.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Account and purchase data: email and payment metadata provided through checkout/payment processors.

- User-provided content: pull request metadata and related text that you choose to analyze.

## 2. How We Use Information

- Provide and operate the Service.
- Process payments and deliver licenses.
- Prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
- Improve reliability, support, and product quality.

## 3. Third-Party Processors

We may use third-party providers (for example payment, hosting, email, and AI providers) to operate the Service. Those providers process data under their own policies and terms.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service operation, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements.

## 5. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Choices

You may request updates or deletion of personal data where applicable by contacting support through repository support channels.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 8. International Processing

Your information may be processed in countries different from your own, where data protection laws may differ.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service after updates means you accept the revised policy.

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the project owner through repository support channels.
